Chicken Malai Tikka

Description

Chicken Malai Tikka (Reshmi Kebab) is a flavorful and tender dish made with succulent chicken chunks marinated in a creamy, spiced yogurt sauce. Whether baked in the oven or grilled over an outdoor BBQ, these juicy kebabs are perfect for summer gatherings. The rich marinade, featuring cream and aromatic spices, ensures the chicken stays moist and full of flavor.

Ideal as a starter, these melt-in-your-mouth chicken malai kebabs pair beautifully with homemade naan or a refreshing salad. They also make an excellent addition to dinner parties or family feasts, offering a delicious twist on classic chicken tikka.
