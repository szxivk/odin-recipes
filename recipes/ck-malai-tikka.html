Chicken Malai Tikka

Description

Chicken Malai Tikka is a flavorful and tender dish made with succulent chicken chunks marinated in a creamy, spiced yogurt sauce. Whether baked in the oven or grilled over an outdoor BBQ, these juicy kebabs are perfect for summer gatherings. The rich marinade, featuring cream and aromatic spices, ensures the chicken stays moist and full of flavor.

Ideal as a starter, these melt-in-your-mouth chicken malai kebabs pair beautifully with homemade naan or a refreshing salad. They also make an excellent addition to dinner parties or family feasts, offering a delicious twist on classic chicken tikka.

Ingredients

  • 500 g Chicken
  • 1-inch Ginger
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 tbsp Yoghurt
  • 2 tbsp Double Cream
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tbsp Cornflour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Dried Fenugreek Leaves
  • 4 Green Chillies (crushed)
  • Coriander (finely chopped)

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into chunks then add into a bowl along with the rest of the ingredients then mix until well combined and set aside to marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Add on to skewers then place on wire rack and at the top of preheated oven at 200°C/400°F/gas mark 6 for 15 minutes.
  3. Flip over then cook for another 15 minutes until cooked through.
  4. Your 402kcal malai chicken tikka is ready!
